1. Backup your SD Card (Copy it to your desktop or some $%#@).
2. Delete your SD Card.
3. Paste the contents of the applicable folder for your firmware version into the root directory of your SD Card.
4. If you know what you're doing re-edit all your custom settings you've edited from your pervious SD card.
5. If you don't know what you're doing you should be fine, but you'll need to perform an extruder step calibration.


NOTE: if you want to upgrade to duet firmware to anything above 3.0 you need to upgrade to 3.0 first then 3.1, 3.2 etc....


For BLTouch addon follow the instructions located in the applicable firmware folder.
